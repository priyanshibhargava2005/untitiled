Paris, the capital of France, is celebrated for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. The city is home to numerous iconic landmarks:​

Eiffel Tower: An iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars, offering panoramic views of the city. ​

Louvre Museum: The world's largest art museum, housing masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo. ​

Notre-Dame Cathedral: A medieval Catholic cathedral known for its French Gothic architecture and historical significance. ​

Champs-Élysées and Arc de Triomphe: A famous avenue leading to a monumental arch honoring those who fought for France. ​
